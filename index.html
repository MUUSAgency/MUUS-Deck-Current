Every brand needs a visual range — hero films can't sound like UGC; UGC shouldn't look like a commercial. We work fluently across eight distinct styles, often blending two or three within a single campaign. Below is the working palette.

Cinematic brand

Lensed, lit, art-directed. Shallow depth, anamorphic when it serves the story. Think hero films, TVCs, premium launches.

Anamorphic Hero TVC

Documentary / verité

Handheld, observational, real moments. Founder content, athlete features, behind-the-scenes that earns trust.

Handheld Founder Long-form
Reference: Athlete features · founder Q&A · gym profile pieces

UGC / creator-led

Self-shot or styled to feel native. Vertical, unpolished by design. Built to read as authentic in feed and convert in paid.

9:16 Whitelisted Native-feeling

Hook-driven CPA-tuned A/B variants

Studio / tabletop

Controlled lighting, motion-controlled rigs, slow-mo on Phantom. Product hero shots, ingredient drops, packaging beauty.

Tabletop Phantom Motion control

Athlete / action capture
